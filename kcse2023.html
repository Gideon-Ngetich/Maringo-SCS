MARINGO MANAGEMENT SYSTEM.

Project Description

Maringo Sports Club was formed by Maringo County with the intention of developing talent among the youths within the county in various games. The club enrols youth of ages 12 to 35 years.The games offered include: swimming, hockey, lawn tennis, table tennis, darts, badminton, volleyball, basketball, netball, football, baseball, rugby, pool, chess, and draft. During registration the youths are required to give accurate personal details. They can enroll as a group or as individuals and are requred to pay a membership fee depending on the type of registration. The registered members are categorised into three: minor, middle group and seniors. The club also sells some sporting equipments to its members at a fee. The club has a clerk who maintains the records of the items sold and stock available also there are patrons who facilitate various sporting activities.
